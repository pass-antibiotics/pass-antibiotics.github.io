Doctor Catherine Smith — Research Associate, UCL Institute of Health Informatics

Catherine Smith is a postdoctoral infectious disease epidemiologist. In the PASS project, Catherine is leading the Bug Watch community cohort study and the analysis of data from care homes. She has experience in research about the application of novel technologies to improving control of infectious diseases. This has included a randomised controlled trial assessing the use of a mobile app to support treatment of tuberculosis, and a study combining epidemiological information with whole genome sequencing to improve understanding of transmission of viruses in hospitals.
