Alongside a meta-analysis of antimicrobial stewardship reviews in hospitals, we have partnered up with Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Birmingham, UK, to conduct primary research. This includes:
  • ethnographic observations on general medical wards,
  • interviews with clinicians,
  • analysis of electronic records of prescribing and test results,
  • engagement with stakeholders on the design and development of future intervention bundles.
